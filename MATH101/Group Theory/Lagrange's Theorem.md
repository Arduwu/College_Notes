Let $d$ be the smallest +ve integer such that $x^{d}=1$.
If there doesnt exists any such $d$ 
Then, we say that $x$ has **infinite order**

Order is denoted as:  $ord(x)$ or $|x|$

***Theorem***
> Let $G$ be a finite group. Then , for any $x \in G$, 
> $ord(x) \leq |G|$
